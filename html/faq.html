Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I sign up?

A: First, head to the Sign up page. Then, you are required to keyin your Username and Password. After that, click on the ‘Sign Up’ button. Poof, your account has successfully been created!

Q: What are the differences between Standard Delivery & Express Delivery?

A: Standard delivery is where you will receive your order 3 to 5 days right after payment; Express delivery is where you will receive your order 1 to 2 days right after payment.

Q: Do you have a physical store?

A: Yes! Please check our physical store address at our website footer. You can also contact us through email or phone call. Check About Us page for more information!

Q: Is your Delivery service cover entire Malaysia?

Unfortunately, no. Our delivery services only cover West Malaysia for now. However, we have partnered with other stores to work in East Malaysia. Drop us an email to know more!

Q: Is there any discounts or offers?

A: Absolutely! There are tons of discounts or offers vouchers from time to time. Do check our website regularly so that you will not miss any chances!
